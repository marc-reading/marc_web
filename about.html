Our Story

MARC is a community of Malayalee families in Reading, UK — connecting hearts and celebrating culture through festivals, family gatherings, youth initiatives, and sports activities.

Who We Are

MARC — Malayalee Association of Reading Community — is a vibrant and close-knit association formed by Malayalee families living in and around Reading, United Kingdom. Established with the vision of bringing people together, MARC serves as a platform to connect families, celebrate shared heritage, and foster a strong sense of belonging within the community.

Our community is built on values of unity, inclusivity, and cultural pride. MARC welcomes families from diverse backgrounds and age groups, creating an environment where traditions are cherished, relationships are strengthened, and friendships grow across generations. Whether it is through festive celebrations, cultural programs, sports activities, or social gatherings, MARC provides opportunities for meaningful engagement and shared experiences.

With the active participation of over 100 Malayalee families, MARC plays an important role in preserving Kerala’s rich cultural traditions while embracing the multicultural spirit of life in the UK. We encourage youth involvement, promote family bonding, and support initiatives that enhance social well-being and community awareness.

Guided by the dedication of our Samithi members and volunteers, MARC continues to evolve as a supportive and inclusive community. Together, we strive to create a welcoming space where members feel connected, valued, and inspired — connecting hearts and celebrating culture, today and for generations to come.
