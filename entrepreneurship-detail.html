Inspire Entrepreneurship

Encouraging a business mindset within the Malayalee community in the UK through real stories, open conversations & meaningful connections.

Saturday, 11 April • 2:00 PM – 6:00 PM • Kennet Valley Community Centre, Reading (RG31 7YT)

Book Your Seat Learn More

Event Details

A focused, community-led gathering

Inspire Entrepreneurship – For the Malayalee Community in the UK is a community-driven event created to encourage a business mindset among Malayalees living in the UK. This event brings together 7–8 successful and friendly entrepreneurs who have built businesses in the UK to share their real-life journeys, including the challenges they faced, the risks they took, the mistakes they learned from, and the breakthroughs that changed their path.

Through short founder stories, open panel discussions , live Q&A and networking opportunities, attendees will gain practical insight into what it truly takes to start and grow a business in the UK. This is not a formal seminar, training course, or awards night. It is a friendly, honest, and supportive space to listen, ask questions, connect, and be inspired.

This is not…

  • This is not a seminar.
  • This is not a gala dinner or awards night.
  • No songs, no dance, no fashion show — just founders, conversations and networking.

Who should attend

  • Professionals exploring business ideas
  • Students & career changers
  • Aspiring entrepreneurs
  • Early-stage founders
  • Anyone curious about business

What to expect

  • Short, honest founder stories
  • Open panel discussion and Q&A
  • Informal networking with speakers and attendees
  • A supportive, positive, business-minded community

Whether you already have a business idea or are just beginning to explore the possibility, this event is designed to give you clarity, confidence, and meaningful connections to take your next step.

Come. Listen. Connect. Grow.
