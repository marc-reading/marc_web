MARC Open Badminton Tournament 2026 (Intermediate Level)

The Malayalee Association of Reading Community (MARC) was established in the early 2010s to fulfil a long-cherished aspiration of the Malayalee (natives of the Indian state of Kerala) community in Reading, UK.

Since its inception, MARC has operated with integrity, inclusiveness, and strong social responsibility, providing a structured platform for Malayalees in and around Reading to connect, collaborate, preserve their moral, social, cultural, educational, literary, and artistic heritage, and actively integrate with UK culture and community life. MARC envisions a sustainable and self-strengthening community that supports present and future generations while preserving cultural identity in a global environment.

With over 85 families, 320+ members, 85+ school-going children, and 60+ youth members, MARC is a vibrant and forward-looking community. Our members contribute meaningfully to UK society, working across healthcare, business, IT, engineering, construction, biotechnology, and many other professional sectors. MARC actively participates in charity initiatives and remains deeply committed to addressing wider social needs.

A flagship initiative of MARC is the UK-wide Badminton Tournament, scheduled for 7th March 2026 at Rivermead Leisure Centre, Reading. Building on the success of the 2025 inaugural event, the 2026 tournament aims to host 120 teams and 240 participants, dedicated to recognising and encouraging sporting talent across the UK. The association embraces inclusivity and diversity, ensuring equal opportunities for participation across all age groups and backgrounds. Beyond sport, MARC is strongly focused on youth empowerment and career development.

We wish all participants the very best for the tournament and extend our heartfelt gratitude to our sponsors for their invaluable support. We cherish this opportunity as a celebration of community building and MARC’s enduring bond with our society.

Sincerely,
MARC Samithi & MARC Badminton Organising Committee
